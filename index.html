How Serapis Works

Serapis is an AI powered knowledge management tool that learns everything about your organization.

STEP ONE

Standardize and Index Your Data

Serapis identifies all of your existing records and unstructured data from sources like SharePoint, OneDrive, network drives, word documents, and emails.

STEP TWO

Build Database of Entities, Actions, and Events

Our tool builds a definitive history of your organization’s actions, people, etc. to date.
